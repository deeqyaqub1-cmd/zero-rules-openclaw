FAQ

Do I need Pro to get the token savings?
No. Free gives you the full 87-95% savings. Pro adds collaboration and management features on top ‚Äî sync, team sharing, web access, version history.
How does multi-workspace sync work?
Install HyperStack in multiple workspaces and connect them to your Pro account. When a card changes in one workspace, it automatically syncs to all others. Conflict resolution is automatic ‚Äî most recent edit wins, with full version history to recover anything.
How does team memory work?
Create a team on cascadeai.dev, invite members. Designate cards as "shared" ‚Äî they appear in every team member's index. Alice updates the deployment card ‚Üí Bob's agent sees it next session. Private cards stay private.
Can't I just copy files between workspaces myself?
You can! Cards are plain markdown. But keeping 30+ cards synced across 4 workspaces and 3 teammates manually? That's a full-time job. Pro makes it invisible.
Where is my data stored?
Cards always live on your machine first. Pro syncs an encrypted copy to our servers for dashboard access and sync. You can delete your cloud copy anytime ‚Äî local files are never affected.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Cancel and your skill keeps working with all your local cards. You lose sync, team sharing, and dashboard access. No data lost, no lock-in.
